# Persona and Roleplay Generation

## Evidence Scope

This synthesis uses the deduplicated `corpus.jsonl` as its citation authority. The merged corpus contains 187 reviewed primary papers: 100 inspected at full-text depth and 87 at abstract depth. Deduplication retained persona-seat IDs for PersonaChat and Dialogue NLI, but retained agent/evaluation-seat IDs for MemoryBank, LoCoMo, InCharacter, and TimeChara; only those merged IDs are cited below.

The most direct evidence concerns assigned personas, fictional or public characters, synthetic long conversations, personalized task benchmarks, and controlled human-simulation tasks. These settings establish bounded role adherence, retrieval, contradiction, and evaluation results, but they do not establish faithful simulation of a consenting living owner across natural relationships ([paper:persona-dialogue-zhang-2018]; [paper:persona-dialogue-wang-2024-rolellm]; [paper:incharacter-2024]; [paper:locomo-2024]; [paper:behaviorchain-2025]).

The strongest mechanism-specific evidence used here is full-text evidence for explicit persona conditioning, dialogue NLI, multi-session memory, role tuning, long-memory benchmarks, individual simulation, behavioral evaluation, and privacy attacks ([paper:persona-dialogue-zhang-2018]; [paper:persona-dialogue-welleck-2019]; [paper:persona-dialogue-xu-2022-goldfish]; [paper:persona-dialogue-shao-2023-character-llm]; [paper:locomo-2024]; [paper:longmemeval-2025]; [paper:generative-agents-1000-2024]; [paper:carlini-2021-training-data-extraction]).

Several useful comparisons remain abstract-depth only: profile-order sensitivity, pragmatic reranking, commonsense expansion, persona graphs, LaMP retrieval, editable memory graphs, and acted-versus-real chat intimacy. Their quantitative and architectural interpretations must remain within the reviewed abstracts ([paper:persona-dialogue-chen-2023-orig]; [paper:persona-dialogue-kim-2020]; [paper:persona-dialogue-majumder-2020]; [paper:persona-dialogue-gao-2023-peacok]; [paper:persona-dialogue-salemi-2024-lamp]; [paper:persona-dialogue-wang-2024-emg-rag]; [paper:persona-dialogue-arimoto-2024]).

The final claim set cites 75 unique merged records: 55 inspected at full-text depth and 20 at abstract depth. Abstract-depth records are used for bounded reported findings or as design motivation, not for uninspected implementation claims.

No metadata-only record is used. Findings from shared datasets are not treated as independent replications, and benchmark success is not promoted into an owner-fidelity claim merely because multiple papers reuse related persona resources ([paper:persona-dialogue-zhang-2018]; [paper:persona-dialogue-welleck-2019]; [paper:persona-dialogue-madotto-2019]).

## VALIDATED Constraints

### Mechanism Comparison

| Approach | What the reviewed evidence supports | Main failure or boundary | Evidence status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Profile prompting | Explicit self-profiles can improve local persona consistency and profile recognizability; keeping the profile inspectable is more controllable than relying only on a latent speaker vector ([paper:persona-dialogue-li-2016]; [paper:persona-dialogue-zhang-2018]). | Own-profile access did not improve engagement in PersonaChat, identical claims can behave differently when reordered, and a static all-time profile can leak future knowledge ([paper:persona-dialogue-zhang-2018]; [paper:persona-dialogue-chen-2023-orig]; [paper:timechara-2024]). | VALIDATED for bounded short-dialogue consistency; not validated as identity reconstruction. |
| Retrieval | Selecting relevant user-history items improves several personalized tasks, and retrieval or read-write memory improves later-session use of prior information ([paper:persona-dialogue-salemi-2024-lamp]; [paper:persona-dialogue-xu-2022-goldfish]; [paper:memgpt-2023]). | Retrieval can miss decisive evidence, retrieve noise, misapply a correct memory, surface stale facts, or make harmful intent look personally legitimate ([paper:memgpt-2023]; [paper:memorybank-2024]; [paper:longmemeval-2025]; [paper:personalization-legitimizes-risks-2026]). | VALIDATED as a useful access mechanism; authorization, correctness, and safety remain separate gates. |
| Graph memory | Typed persona relations and entity-event links can organize characteristics, goals, experiences, relationships, people, places, time, and media; editable graph nodes offer a correction surface ([paper:persona-dialogue-gao-2023-peacok]; [paper:connected-memories-comet-2022]; [paper:persona-dialogue-wang-2024-emg-rag]). | Generic commonsense edges are not owner facts, COMET uses synthetic memories, and the editable-memory result is abstract-depth with unverified source-provenance and deletion details ([paper:persona-dialogue-gao-2023-peacok]; [paper:connected-memories-comet-2022]; [paper:persona-dialogue-wang-2024-emg-rag]). | PLAUSIBLE as an inspectable index; not validated as a complete personal-memory substrate. |
| Summarization | Summary memory can improve later-session topic use, and hierarchical summaries or topic segments can reduce prompt load while retaining useful conversational information ([paper:persona-dialogue-xu-2022-goldfish]; [paper:memorybank-2024]; [paper:secom-2025]). | Summaries are lossy derived artifacts; retrieval success does not guarantee correct use, and reflection systems have fabricated embellishments when source retrieval failed ([paper:memorybank-2024]; [paper:secom-2025]; [paper:generative-agents-2023]). | VALIDATED as compression in controlled tasks; rejected as the canonical record. |
| Tuning or adapters | Meta-learning, character-specific tuning, and role-conditioned instruction tuning can improve bounded persona or role scores and can teach some knowledge-boundary behavior ([paper:persona-dialogue-madotto-2019]; [paper:persona-dialogue-shao-2023-character-llm]; [paper:persona-dialogue-wang-2024-rolellm]). | The evidence uses assigned or public characters, generated training scenes, and mostly short tasks; persona fine-tuning can reduce open-domain coherence, while the reviewed training-data evidence shows that learned text can be difficult to inspect, delete, and protect from extraction ([paper:persona-dialogue-shao-2023-character-llm]; [paper:persona-dialogue-xu-2022-lemon]; [paper:carlini-2021-training-data-extraction]; [paper:bourtoule-2021-machine-unlearning]). | VALIDATED for bounded role adaptation; owner-specific tuning remains a constrained hypothesis. |
| Long context | Larger contexts can improve long-history question answering and can complement retrieval when relevant evidence remains visible ([paper:locomo-2024]; [paper:longmemeval-2025]). | Long-context systems remain far below humans on LoCoMo, degrade over sustained histories, struggle with temporal, adversarial, update, and abstention cases, and expose more context that can unlock memorized sequences ([paper:locomo-2024]; [paper:longmemeval-2025]; [paper:quantifying-memorization-2023]). | VALIDATED as one access path; rejected as a substitute for memory structure and governance. |

### Cross-Cutting Constraints

1. **Local consistency is not identity fidelity.** Speaker vectors, explicit profiles, and few-shot adaptation can improve short-range consistency, but the experiments reward assigned-role adherence or next-response behavior rather than reconstruction of a living person's identity ([paper:persona-dialogue-li-2016]; [paper:persona-dialogue-zhang-2018]; [paper:persona-dialogue-madotto-2019]).

2. **Conditioning does not make generation contradiction-proof.** Dialogue-domain NLI and pragmatic reranking reduce explicit contradictions, but absence of contradiction is not positive grounding and sentence-pair checks do not cover multi-hop, temporal, relational, or pragmatic incompatibility ([paper:persona-dialogue-welleck-2019]; [paper:persona-dialogue-kim-2020]).

3. **Time must be represented, not inferred from prompt position.** Role agents leak future knowledge, long-memory systems miss updates and temporal relations, and identical survey prompts can drift after a deployed model update ([paper:timechara-2024]; [paper:longmemeval-2025]; [paper:synthetic-replacements-2024]).

4. **A persona is context- and relationship-conditioned.** Significant-other cues can activate relationship-specific self-knowledge, people present themselves differently to friends and strangers, and turn-level accommodation depends on direction and discourse rather than a single global style score ([paper:andersen-chen-2002-relational-self]; [paper:tice-et-al-1995-when-modesty-prevails]; [paper:danescu-niculescu-mizil-et-al-2011-mark-my-words]; [paper:doyle-frank-2016-sources-linguistic-alignment]).

5. **Stable and transient behavior must remain separate.** Broad traits require aggregation across occasions, within-person trait-relevant states vary widely, and both person and situation independently contribute to observed behavior ([paper:psychometrics_epstein_1983]; [paper:psychometrics_fleeson_2001]; [paper:psychometrics_sherman_2015]).

6. **Long-horizon errors compound.** BehaviorChain's low cumulative chain score shows that an early generated action can corrupt later predictions, while long-memory benchmarks show persistent retrieval, update, and abstention failures across sustained histories ([paper:behaviorchain-2025]; [paper:longmemeval-2025]).

7. **Fidelity is multidimensional.** Knowledge, personality, behavior, style, social goals, rapport, temporal knowledge, and human agreement separate empirically; a model can recognize persona cues better than it can generate persona-consistent behavior ([paper:charactereval-2024]; [paper:sotopia-2024]; [paper:timechara-2024]; [paper:twinvoice-2026]).

8. **Plausibility and aggregate fit are weak proxies for individual behavior.** Synthetic communities can look real without being predictions, survey-conditioned agents can perform well on some held-out items while missing effects or normative deviations, and prompted shopping agents remain poor at exact next actions ([paper:social-simulacra-2022]; [paper:generative-agents-1000-2024]; [paper:twin-2k-500-2025]; [paper:shopcart-human-behavior-2026]).

9. **Memorization can masquerade as personal specificity.** Extractable text and PII increase with duplication, context, and model scale; ordinary alignment or refusal behavior does not prove that memorized data is absent ([paper:kandpal-2022-dedup-privacy]; [paper:quantifying-memorization-2023]; [paper:carlini-2021-training-data-extraction]; [paper:lukas-2023-pii-leakage]; [paper:aligned-production-extraction-2025]).

10. **Deceptive impersonation is not validated as a legitimate fidelity target.** Roleplay papers evaluate public or fictional characters rather than authorization to represent a living owner, and community-level plausibility does not establish that a generated identity is authentic ([paper:persona-dialogue-shao-2023-character-llm]; [paper:persona-dialogue-wang-2024-rolellm]; [paper:incharacter-2024]; [paper:social-simulacra-2022]).

11. **Provenance controls do not by themselves prove authorization.** Voice watermarking and face-manipulation detection are bounded by generator integration, compression, unseen attacks, and detector access; neither a watermark nor a detector score establishes consent or identity ([paper:san-roman-2024-audioseal]; [paper:rossler-2019-faceforensics]).

12. **Posthumous stewardship is not permission to personate.** Limited account stewardship, delegate expectation mismatch, and anticipated griefbot harms support explicit advance directives and visible simulation boundaries rather than inherited authority to produce new statements as the person ([paper:brubaker-2016-legacy-contact]; [paper:gach-2021-facebook-affairs]; [paper:lu-2026-griefbot-perceptions]).

## PLAUSIBLE Design Hypotheses

1. **Use a hybrid context compiler with explicit jobs for each mechanism.** A current role/profile prompt should state active policy; retrieval should supply mutable episodes and claims; a graph should connect entities, time, relationships, and provenance; summaries should compress but never replace evidence; an optional adapter should encode only versioned stable style; and long context should carry the bounded working set ([paper:persona-dialogue-zhang-2018]; [paper:persona-dialogue-salemi-2024-lamp]; [paper:connected-memories-comet-2022]; [paper:secom-2025]; [paper:persona-dialogue-wang-2024-rolellm]; [paper:locomo-2024]).

2. **Keep canonical identity outside model weights.** A source-linked event ledger plus versioned semantic, relationship, and narrative views is more inspectable and correctable than a latent speaker embedding or per-owner fine-tune, although no included paper evaluates this complete architecture end to end ([paper:persona-dialogue-li-2016]; [paper:persona-dialogue-xu-2022-goldfish]; [paper:memorybank-2024]; [paper:bourtoule-2021-machine-unlearning]).

3. **Compile the active persona after building a situation model.** The request, target time, relationship, audience, role, task, and permission state should determine which owner evidence is eligible before any profile text is serialized ([paper:timechara-2024]; [paper:andersen-chen-2002-relational-self]; [paper:litt-hargittai-2016-imagined-audience]; [paper:mireshghallah-2024-confaide]).

4. **Separate global owner state from relationship overlays.** Global facts and owner-confirmed commitments should coexist with relationship-scoped common ground, self-presentation, style, and disclosure rules; cross-relationship differences should remain scoped observations rather than be resolved into one supposedly true persona ([paper:brennan-clark-1996-conceptual-pacts]; [paper:tice-et-al-1995-when-modesty-prevails]; [paper:lampinen-et-al-2011-were-in-it-together]).

5. **Version drift instead of silently overwriting it.** Stable, slowly changing, episodic, and request-local components should update on different schedules; new behavior should first become evidence, then a proposed typed delta, then a tested and reversible state change ([paper:koren-2009-temporal-dynamics]; [paper:mi-2020-ader]; [paper:lopez-paz-2017-gem]; [paper:masson-dautume-2019-episodic-language-memory]).

6. **Do not let generated behavior become autobiographical evidence.** Generated plans, inferred motives, summaries, and roleplay outputs should live in a simulation namespace until independently confirmed, because reflection can embellish memories, source reactivation can contaminate recall, and a wrong early behavior can propagate through a chain ([paper:generative-agents-2023]; [paper:cognitive-memory-hupbach-et-al-2007]; [paper:behaviorchain-2025]).

7. **Run access control before retrieval and safety after retrieval.** Contextual permissions must prevent unauthorized memories from entering the prompt, while the safety gate must inspect the same retrieved context as the generator because benign persona evidence can legitimize harmful requests ([paper:mireshghallah-2024-confaide]; [paper:personalization-legitimizes-risks-2026]).

8. **Treat owner correction as authority but preview its model effect.** Owner edits should be typed as correction, supersession, contextual exception, do-not-use, or deletion; previews and rollback can reduce accidental broad profile damage without overruling the owner's decision ([paper:ahn-2007-open-user-profiles]; [paper:bourtoule-2021-machine-unlearning]).

9. **Use a release boundary, not an impersonation score.** Outputs should be visibly labeled as generated simulation, carry the evidence cutoff and provenance trace, avoid claims that the living or deceased person authored the response, and prohibit autonomous commitments on their behalf; the literature supports the need for provenance and authorization but does not validate a complete text-impersonation defense ([paper:san-roman-2024-audioseal]; [paper:brubaker-2016-legacy-contact]; [paper:lu-2026-griefbot-perceptions]).

## SPECULATIVE Claims

1. A graph-plus-retrieval hybrid may improve owner-rated long-horizon consistency over profile prompting alone, but the graph papers use generic, synthetic, or abstract-level evidence and do not test a consented integrated life history ([paper:persona-dialogue-gao-2023-peacok]; [paper:connected-memories-comet-2022]; [paper:persona-dialogue-wang-2024-emg-rag]).

2. A small owner adapter may learn durable communication style without storing mutable facts, but the reviewed tuning studies use assigned/public characters and do not establish deletion, cross-relationship transfer, or resistance to private-text memorization ([paper:persona-dialogue-madotto-2019]; [paper:persona-dialogue-shao-2023-character-llm]; [paper:persona-dialogue-wang-2024-rolellm]; [paper:carlini-2021-training-data-extraction]).

3. Periodic summarization or reflection may improve autobiographical continuity, but it may also omit counterexamples, amplify frequently retrieved claims, or introduce unsupported interpretation; no reviewed study validates a safe consolidation schedule for a real owner ([paper:memorybank-2024]; [paper:generative-agents-2023]; [paper:cognitive-memory-anderson-bjork-bjork-1994]; [paper:cognitive-memory-hupbach-et-al-2007]).

4. Relationship-conditioned style generation may feel more authentic than a global style vector, but automatic accommodation can become caricature, imitate a correspondent, or mistake discourse effects for identity unless owner-scoped tests succeed ([paper:persona-dialogue-li-2016]; [paper:danescu-niculescu-mizil-et-al-2011-mark-my-words]; [paper:doyle-frank-2016-sources-linguistic-alignment]).

5. WhatsApp-only evidence may be sufficient for channel-conditioned reply simulation in some relationships, but it is not established as sufficient for values, motives, offline behavior, a global personality, or a complete digital self ([paper:psychometrics_fleeson_2001]; [paper:psychometrics_sherman_2015]; [paper:persona-dialogue-arimoto-2024]; [paper:shopcart-human-behavior-2026]).

6. Visible disclosure, watermarking, and provenance may reduce some deceptive use, but the corpus does not contain a longitudinal text-impersonation deployment study showing that these controls prevent social deception, coercion, or unauthorized representation ([paper:san-roman-2024-audioseal]; [paper:rossler-2019-faceforensics]; [paper:lu-2026-griefbot-perceptions]).

## Contradictions and Boundaries

| Tension | Positive evidence | Negative, null, or boundary evidence | Synthesis boundary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Persona adherence versus conversation quality | Explicit profiles improved consistency and detectability ([paper:persona-dialogue-zhang-2018]). | Own-profile access did not improve engagement, and arbitrary sentence order changed behavior ([paper:persona-dialogue-zhang-2018]; [paper:persona-dialogue-chen-2023-orig]). | Report adherence and engagement separately; do not rank prompt layouts as identity strength. |
| Retrieval utility versus safety | Retrieved history improved personalization and later-session memory ([paper:persona-dialogue-salemi-2024-lamp]; [paper:persona-dialogue-xu-2022-goldfish]). | Retrieved benign memories increased harmful-query success in PS-Bench ([paper:personalization-legitimizes-risks-2026]). | Retrieval is neither authorization nor safety; gate before and after context assembly. |
| Summary efficiency versus evidence fidelity | Summary memory and topic segmentation improved controlled long-memory tasks ([paper:persona-dialogue-xu-2022-goldfish]; [paper:secom-2025]). | Summaries are lossy, wrong retrieval can be misapplied, and reflection can embellish memories ([paper:memorybank-2024]; [paper:generative-agents-2023]). | Preserve source segments and treat summaries as reversible views. |
| Tuning gains versus generality and deletion | Role and meta-learning improved bounded persona scores ([paper:persona-dialogue-madotto-2019]; [paper:persona-dialogue-wang-2024-rolellm]). | Persona task tuning reduced open-domain coherence, generated scenes can contain fiction, and learned artifacts complicate deletion and leakage ([paper:persona-dialogue-xu-2022-lemon]; [paper:persona-dialogue-shao-2023-character-llm]; [paper:bourtoule-2021-machine-unlearning]; [paper:carlini-2021-training-data-extraction]). | Keep mutable identity out of weights and require adapter ablation, rollback, and extraction tests. |
| Survey fit versus real behavior | Rich self-reports improved some held-out survey predictions over demographics ([paper:generative-agents-1000-2024]; [paper:twin-2k-500-2025]). | Economic-game agent types did not significantly differ, several causal effects failed, and real shopping next-action accuracy remained low ([paper:generative-agents-1000-2024]; [paper:twin-2k-500-2025]; [paper:shopcart-human-behavior-2026]). | Keep domains separate and prioritize held-out observed behavior over plausible explanation. |
| Psychometric consistency versus human-like structure | Interview assessment can align with static fictional-character labels ([paper:incharacter-2024]). | Emulated profiles can be unnaturally factor-pure, socially desirable, and unstable across model, trait, instrument, or date ([paper:emulate-personality-2025]; [paper:personality-temporal-stability-2024]). | Test covariance, criterion validity, context, and temporal reliability, not only target scores. |
| Synthetic realism versus natural relationships | Roleplay and simulated communities can be coherent and plausible ([paper:persona-dialogue-wang-2024-rolellm]; [paper:social-simulacra-2022]). | Acted long-term chats showed an unnatural intimacy pattern, and TwinVoice's dimensions do not form one person's integrated life ([paper:persona-dialogue-arimoto-2024]; [paper:twinvoice-2026]). | Treat synthetic benchmarks as diagnostics, not ecological validation. |
| More retained evidence versus more fidelity | Long histories can improve retrieval opportunities ([paper:locomo-2024]; [paper:longmemeval-2025]). | Accuracy still degrades, selective retrieval can suppress competitors, and duplicated/context-rich text increases extraction risk ([paper:longmemeval-2025]; [paper:cognitive-memory-anderson-bjork-bjork-1994]; [paper:quantifying-memorization-2023]). | Optimize calibrated use and retention, not maximum storage or prompt length. |
| Owner control versus predictive metrics | Users value inspectable and editable profiles ([paper:ahn-2007-open-user-profiles]). | Editing reduced objective precision and recall in the small YourNews study ([paper:ahn-2007-open-user-profiles]). | Owner authority is not conditional on metric gain; preview scope and preserve rollback. |
| Global consistency versus relational authenticity | Profile-grounded NLI reranking can reduce contradictions ([paper:persona-dialogue-welleck-2019]). | Relationship cues, audience, and discourse produce legitimate variation across contexts ([paper:andersen-chen-2002-relational-self]; [paper:tice-et-al-1995-when-modesty-prevails]; [paper:doyle-frank-2016-sources-linguistic-alignment]). | A contradiction must be evaluated against time and relationship scope before it is repaired. |

## Architecture Consequences

1. **Canonical evidence ledger.** Store authorized source events with speaker/subject, audience, source time, observed time, relationship, modality, consent scope, retention state, and tamper-evident source lineage; retention or deletion policy must remain executable across derived artifacts rather than pretending model weights are the source of truth ([paper:mireshghallah-2024-confaide]; [paper:franz-2022-interdependent-privacy]; [paper:bourtoule-2021-machine-unlearning]).

2. **Typed derived state.** Keep episodes, semantic claims, self-schema, relationship common ground, style distributions, goals, narrative versions, summaries, and simulations in separate namespaces with links back to evidence ([paper:cognitive-memory-vargha-khadem-1997]; [paper:brennan-clark-1996-conceptual-pacts]; [paper:memorybank-2024]).

3. **Time and supersession.** Every claim needs observation time, valid-from, valid-to, supersedes, contradicted-by, and knowledge-cutoff fields; retrieval for a past simulation must exclude later knowledge while preserving the historical record ([paper:timechara-2024]; [paper:longmemeval-2025]; [paper:koren-2009-temporal-dynamics]).

4. **Relationship and audience scope.** The active situation model must distinguish global owner evidence from relationship-specific aliases, self-presentation, accommodation, intended audience, permitted audience, and third-party disclosure constraints ([paper:andersen-chen-2002-relational-self]; [paper:litt-hargittai-2016-imagined-audience]; [paper:lampinen-et-al-2011-were-in-it-together]).

5. **Permission-aware retrieval.** Filter by subject, recipient, purpose, relationship, time, and capability before lexical, semantic, temporal, or graph retrieval; include a no-use result when evidence is absent, stale, conflicting, or unauthorized ([paper:persona-dialogue-salemi-2024-lamp]; [paper:mireshghallah-2024-confaide]; [paper:longmemeval-2025]).

6. **Source-preserving compression.** Segment coherent episodes and generate summaries or reflections as versioned views that retain source pointers, omitted-evidence diagnostics, and invalidation when underlying claims change ([paper:secom-2025]; [paper:persona-dialogue-xu-2022-goldfish]; [paper:generative-agents-2023]).

7. **Explicit prompt assembly.** Serialize only the current role, policy, relevant claims, uncertainty, and permitted evidence; canonicalize unordered claim sets and make priority a function of confidence, scope, and time rather than sentence order ([paper:persona-dialogue-chen-2023-orig]; [paper:persona-dialogue-zhang-2018]).

8. **Optional style adapter.** Permit a versioned adapter only after stable cross-time and cross-relationship patterns survive held-out tests; exclude mutable facts, third-party text, and unconfirmed inferences, and retain the training manifest and deletion path ([paper:persona-dialogue-madotto-2019]; [paper:psychometrics_fleeson_2001]; [paper:bourtoule-2021-machine-unlearning]).

9. **Independent response gate.** Before release, test evidence grounding, contradiction, temporal leakage, relationship scope, authorization, unsupported certainty, harmful personalization, and verbatim private-text leakage ([paper:persona-dialogue-welleck-2019]; [paper:timechara-2024]; [paper:personalization-legitimizes-risks-2026]; [paper:carlini-2021-training-data-extraction]).

10. **Trace and disclosure.** The runtime trace should distinguish quoted evidence, grounded summary, inference, assumption, selected role policy, model generation, rejected candidates, and final uncertainty; the user-facing output must remain visibly synthetic and must not claim the owner authored it ([paper:linguistic-calibration-2022]; [paper:lu-2026-griefbot-perceptions]).

11. **Quarantined write-back.** Generated responses, inferred motives, graph expansions, and summaries cannot become owner evidence automatically; they may create a proposed delta that is regression-tested and, when consequential, owner-confirmed ([paper:generative-agents-2023]; [paper:cognitive-memory-hupbach-et-al-2007]; [paper:lopez-paz-2017-gem]).

12. **Inspectable deletion.** Deletion must propagate through source rows, summaries, graph edges, embeddings, retrieval caches, replay sets, adapters, checkpoints, and released artifacts, followed by leakage and behavior audits ([paper:bourtoule-2021-machine-unlearning]; [paper:shokri-2017-membership-inference]; [paper:lukas-2023-pii-leakage]).

## Evaluation Tests

| Test | Procedure and required comparison | Failure signal | Evidence basis |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Profile value and robustness | Compare no-profile, explicit profile, paraphrased profile, and shuffled-profile conditions; score consistency, recognizability, engagement, and order variance separately. | A profile gain disappears under paraphrase or order permutation, or adherence rises while engagement falls. | [paper:persona-dialogue-zhang-2018]; [paper:persona-dialogue-chen-2023-orig] |
| Retrieval ablation | Compare no retrieval, lexical, semantic, time-aware, graph-assisted, and oracle retrieval; score retrieval recall, source correctness, answer correctness, and abstention separately. | The right evidence is missed, unauthorized evidence enters context, or a retrieved item is used incorrectly. | [paper:persona-dialogue-salemi-2024-lamp]; [paper:locomo-2024]; [paper:memorybank-2024]; [paper:mireshghallah-2024-confaide] |
| Graph edit and provenance | Compare flat retrieval with entity-event and persona graphs; inject wrong edges, apply owner correction/deletion, and verify source-level propagation. | Graph traversal improves task score but cannot identify, correct, supersede, or delete the supporting source. | [paper:connected-memories-comet-2022]; [paper:persona-dialogue-gao-2023-peacok]; [paper:persona-dialogue-wang-2024-emg-rag] |
| Summary faithfulness | Compare source turns, topic segments, short summaries, and hierarchical summaries on later recall, contradictions, omissions, and correction invalidation. | Compression hides counterevidence, changes attribution, or remains active after its source is corrected. | [paper:persona-dialogue-xu-2022-goldfish]; [paper:secom-2025]; [paper:memorybank-2024] |
| Tuning versus retrieval | Compare base prompt, retrieval-only, adapter-only, and retrieval-plus-adapter on held-out time and relationship splits; include general-coherence and rollback tests. | Role score improves while coherence, cross-context behavior, deletion, or current-fact accuracy regresses. | [paper:persona-dialogue-madotto-2019]; [paper:persona-dialogue-xu-2022-lemon]; [paper:persona-dialogue-shao-2023-character-llm] |
| Long-context stress | Vary history length and distractor density; include temporal ordering, causal links, superseded facts, adversarial premises, unanswerable questions, and context-seeded extraction probes. | Accuracy collapses with history length, future facts leak backward, or longer context unlocks private text. | [paper:locomo-2024]; [paper:longmemeval-2025]; [paper:quantifying-memorization-2023] |
| Multi-turn drift | Hold the same persona, relationship, and cutoff across long conversations and model versions; measure contradiction, style drift, knowledge leakage, cumulative chain accuracy, and recovery after a corrected branch. | Early generated errors become later history or the same frozen setup changes after a model update. | [paper:behaviorchain-2025]; [paper:timechara-2024]; [paper:synthetic-replacements-2024] |
| Relationship fidelity | Test the same intent across owner-approved friends, family, work, stranger, and mixed-audience scenarios; separate symmetric similarity from directional accommodation. | A global style wins while relationship boundaries, discourse acts, or partner common ground are violated. | [paper:tice-et-al-1995-when-modesty-prevails]; [paper:danescu-niculescu-mizil-et-al-2011-mark-my-words]; [paper:doyle-frank-2016-sources-linguistic-alignment] |
| Multidimensional role quality | Score factual knowledge, personality, behavior, linguistic style, rapport, goal completion, temporal knowledge, and human/owner agreement as separate axes. | One strong automatic score hides poor behavior, social judgment, or owner recognition. | [paper:charactereval-2024]; [paper:incharacter-2024]; [paper:sotopia-2024]; [paper:twinvoice-2026] |
| Held-out behavioral validity | Reserve later owner responses, choices, and actions; compare demographics-only, recency-only, retrieval-only, and full-system baselines; normalize where possible by owner test-retest reliability. | Fluent answers match population means but miss the owner's later behavior, variance, or causal response. | [paper:generative-agents-1000-2024]; [paper:twin-2k-500-2025]; [paper:shopcart-human-behavior-2026] |
| Drift and update retention | Evaluate new-period utility, old-period retention, rare owner-approved anchors, forward transfer, backward transfer, and rollback for each update class. | A preservation mechanism hurts a stable stream, or a new update erases a rare but owner-important commitment. | [paper:mi-2020-ader]; [paper:kirkpatrick-2017-ewc]; [paper:lopez-paz-2017-gem] |

## WhatsApp Observability Gaps

No WhatsApp content or private owner data was accessed for this synthesis.

1. A sent message is relationship-conditioned behavior, not a context-free persona observation; friend/stranger presentation, partner-specific wording, and discourse-conditioned accommodation can all produce legitimate variation ([paper:tice-et-al-1995-when-modesty-prevails]; [paper:brennan-clark-1996-conceptual-pacts]; [paper:doyle-frank-2016-sources-linguistic-alignment]).

2. First-person text may be a joke, quotation, aspiration, temporary state, copied text, or performance; web-scale persona extraction itself retained temporary and contradictory first-person statements ([paper:persona-dialogue-mazare-2018]; [paper:persona-dialogue-majumder-2020]).

3. Message timestamps show when text was sent, not when a belief became true, stopped being true, or was privately revised; all-time retrieval can therefore create future-knowledge and supersession errors ([paper:timechara-2024]; [paper:longmemeval-2025]).

4. Direct recipients and group membership do not reveal the imagined audience, screenshots, forwarding, offline retelling, or who the owner expected would hear about the message ([paper:litt-hargittai-2016-imagined-audience]; [paper:vitak-2012-context-collapse-privacy]).

5. The archive does not expose unsent alternatives, offline actions, abandoned plans, or the outcome needed to distinguish an expressed preference from actual behavior; plausible simulation can therefore remain behaviorally wrong ([paper:llm-user-sim-crs-2024]; [paper:shopcart-human-behavior-2026]).

6. Silence is not negation, forgetting, or preference drift; broad traits require repeated observations across contexts, and retrieval accessibility can change independently of truth ([paper:psychometrics_epstein_1983]; [paper:psychometrics_fleeson_2001]; [paper:cognitive-memory-anderson-bjork-bjork-1994]).

7. A correspondent's words, identity, relationship details, images, and inferred attributes are not owned solely by the archive holder; contextual disclosure and interdependent privacy require subject-, audience-, purpose-, and time-scoped permissions ([paper:franz-2022-interdependent-privacy]; [paper:mireshghallah-2024-confaide]).

8. Repetition can reflect a durable pattern, forwarding, quotation, backup duplication, or platform convention; treating repetition as tuning weight can amplify both false persona claims and memorization risk ([paper:kandpal-2022-dedup-privacy]; [paper:quantifying-memorization-2023]).

9. Repeated tellings can change meaning without proving factual change or deception, and elicitation can immediately shift self-description; narrative versions need owner adjudication rather than automatic reconciliation ([paper:jardmo-et-al-2023-repeated-narratives]; [paper:dargembeau-garcia-jimenez-2024-working-self]).

10. WhatsApp history supplies no native owner test-retest ceiling, counterfactual choice, or independent criterion for whether a generated reply reflects the owner rather than archive imitation; separate prospective evaluation is required ([paper:generative-agents-1000-2024]; [paper:twin-2k-500-2025]).

## Highest-Value Owner Questions

1. **Which statements are owner-confirmed facts, and which are jokes, quotes, aspirations, temporary states, or old beliefs?** This determines whether text enters evidence, hypothesis, or expired-state layers ([paper:persona-dialogue-mazare-2018]; [paper:timechara-2024]).

2. **Which facts and styles are global, and which apply only to a specific relationship, role, audience, language, or channel?** This prevents one relationship's self-presentation from becoming the global persona ([paper:andersen-chen-2002-relational-self]; [paper:tice-et-al-1995-when-modesty-prevails]).

3. **Which memories may be retrieved for which recipient, purpose, and capability, and which third-party material must be excluded or redacted?** Prompt-level privacy reminders are insufficient when unauthorized content has already entered context ([paper:franz-2022-interdependent-privacy]; [paper:mireshghallah-2024-confaide]).

4. **Which changes are corrections, genuine preference drift, relationship-specific exceptions, temporary suppression, or deletion?** These update types need different validity and rollback behavior ([paper:koren-2009-temporal-dynamics]; [paper:ahn-2007-open-user-profiles]).

5. **Which owner-approved episodes or commitments are rare but must survive updates?** Frequency-based replay and selective retrieval can underprotect infrequent evidence ([paper:mi-2020-ader]; [paper:cognitive-memory-anderson-bjork-bjork-1994]).

6. **Which communication patterns are stable enough to consider for an adapter, and what evidence would require retraining or unlearning it?** Bounded tuning gains do not resolve cross-context stability, deletion, or extraction risk ([paper:persona-dialogue-madotto-2019]; [paper:bourtoule-2021-machine-unlearning]; [paper:carlini-2021-training-data-extraction]).

7. **When should the simulator abstain, ask the owner, or give several plausible owner-consistent responses?** Temporal, causal, and unanswerable long-memory cases remain difficult even for strong systems ([paper:locomo-2024]; [paper:longmemeval-2025]).

8. **What counts as an acceptable simulation: recognizable style, factual recall, relationship appropriateness, later-response prediction, decision fidelity, or some weighted set?** These constructs do not collapse into one score ([paper:charactereval-2024]; [paper:sotopia-2024]; [paper:generative-agents-1000-2024]).

9. **Which actions must the system never take or phrase as the owner, even when confidence is high?** Plausibility, role skill, watermarking, or detection does not confer authority to impersonate or commit on a person's behalf ([paper:social-simulacra-2022]; [paper:san-roman-2024-audioseal]; [paper:brubaker-2016-legacy-contact]).

10. **What should happen after death or incapacity, and who may preserve, delete, export, or simulate each modality?** Stewardship, simulation, voice, face, audience, duration, and revocation require separate advance choices ([paper:brubaker-2016-legacy-contact]; [paper:gach-2021-facebook-affairs]; [paper:lu-2026-griefbot-perceptions]).
